ABOUT THE EVENT

Defunct is an online technology event that celebrates defunct, un-loved, and forgotten technologies. A collection of short lightning talks, given by incredible speakers, will cast a humorous and sometimes poignant light on the technology that built the modern world. The talks are no longer than 10 minutes each and will discuss a technology that is no longer widely used but was once an essential or promising part of the technology scene.
